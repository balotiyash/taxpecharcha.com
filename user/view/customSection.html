The Customs Act, 1962


The Customs Act, 1962, is India's primary law governing customs duties.
It regulates the import and export of goods, ensuring compliance with customs procedures.
The Act facilitates the collection of duties and prevents illegal trade activities,
playing a crucial role in border control and revenue generation.
