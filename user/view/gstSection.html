The Goods and Services Tax Act, 2017


The Goods and Services Tax (GST) Act, 2017, is India's unified indirect tax law.
It combines taxes like VAT, service tax, and excise duty into a single system to simplify taxation.
Implemented on July 1, 2017, the GST Act standardizes the tax structure across the country and
eliminates the cascading effect of multiple taxes.
