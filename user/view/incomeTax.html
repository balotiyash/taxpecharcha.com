The Income-tax Act, 1961


The Income-tax Act, 1961 is the charging statute of Income Tax in India.
It provides for levy, administration, collection and recovery of Income Tax.
The Government of India brought a draft statute called the "Direct Taxes Code"
intended to replace the Income Tax Act, 1961 and the Wealth Tax Act, 1957.
